    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the process for enrolling in a course, and what do I need to do to get started?

    To enroll in a course, simply click the "Enroll" button on the course page and follow the prompts to complete the enrollment process. You will need to create an account on our platform if you haven't already done so.

    How long do I have to complete a course and can I work at my own pace?

    The length of time to complete a course varies depending on the course. You can find the course duration on the course page. However, most of our courses are self-paced, so you can complete them at your own speed and on your own schedule.

    What is your refund policy for courses, and how do I go about requesting a refund?

    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for our courses. If you are unsatisfied with the course, please contact us within 30 days of enrollment to request a refund.
